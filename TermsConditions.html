Logo Redesign – Terms and Conditions

1. Service Scope

This service is strictly offered to businesses that already have an existing logo. The purpose of this service is to refine, modernize, or enhance a current logo design. Businesses without an existing logo must request a separate logo creation service before a redesign can be undertaken.

2. Pricing Structure

The first logo redesign service provided to a business is offered at no cost. This includes up to five (5) revisions as outlined in this agreement.

If the business exceeds the allocated five (5) revisions during the first redesign, a non-refundable deposit will be required before any additional revisions are completed.

For any subsequent logo redesign services (including second, third, or future requests), a non-refundable deposit will be required upfront before work begins.

3. Ownership

All design work remains the property of the service provider until the project has been fully completed, all applicable conditions have been met, and a review has been provided by the business.

Upon completion of the project and fulfillment of these conditions, full ownership rights of the final approved logo design will be transferred to the business.

4. Revisions

The logo redesign package includes a maximum of five (5) revisions. These revisions are intended to ensure alignment with the business’s vision and expectations.

Any revisions beyond the included five (5) will be treated as additional work and will require a non-refundable deposit before further work can proceed.

5. Additional Work & Payment

Any work beyond the included scope will be treated as an additional service. A non-refundable deposit is required upfront, and no further work will begin until payment has been received.

6. Engagement Requirement

The business is required to remain responsive and engaged throughout the design process.

If no communication or feedback is received within 48 hours during an active stage of the project, the project may be paused or closed at the discretion of the service provider. Resuming the project may require a new agreement or additional fees.

7. Approval & Final Delivery

Once the business approves the final logo design, the project will be considered complete. Any further changes requested after approval will be treated as a new service.

Final files will only be delivered once all conditions have been met, including any required payments and submission of a review.

8. Portfolio Rights

The service provider reserves the right to showcase the completed logo design in their portfolio, marketing materials, and promotional content.

9. Communication Hours

Business hours are strictly from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM. All communication must take place within these hours. Messages received outside of business hours will be addressed on the next working day.

10. Non-Refundable Policy

All payments made, including deposits for additional or future work, are non-refundable.

11. No Rush Services

Rush services are not offered. All work is completed within standard timelines as communicated at the start of the project.

12. Right to Refuse Service

The service provider reserves the right to refuse or terminate a project if communication becomes unprofessional, requirements fall outside the agreed scope, or these terms are not respected.
